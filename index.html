FSRDC Research Output Dashboard

This dashboard summarizes key insights from our CIT 5900 Project 3 analysis of Federal Statistical Research Data Center (FSRDC) outputs. Interactive charts let you explore the data — captions highlight the main take-aways.

Top 10 Research Data Centers (RDCs)

Boston, Washington DC, and Michigan currently lead in total outputs, each exceeding 140 publications.

Counts Over Years

Output volume climbed from single-digits in the early 1990s to a record 126 publications in 2018.

Publication Trends Over Time

FSRDC publications grew steadily until 2018, dipped during the COVID-19 period, and rebounded sharply in 2023.

Top 10 Authors

The most prolific researchers each have 25+ verified outputs; Kevin McKinney leads the list with 38.

Additional Insight — Topic × Citations

Efficiency & productivity papers accumulate citations faster than demographic studies, even after adjusting for publication age.

Yearly Output Counts (Our Dataset vs FSRDC‑Verified 2024)

Distribution of Output Types

Journal‑article publications account for ~60 % of new outputs, whereas the verified dataset remains heavier on working papers. Source: Fig. 4.citeturn5file15
